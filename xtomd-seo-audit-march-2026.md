## Executive Summary

**xtomd.com has a strong technical foundation for a day-one launch.** The site loads fast on Cloudflare's edge network, has clean URL structures, proper Schema.org markup (WebApplication + FAQPage), and well-optimized title tags on all four pages. For a brand-new domain launched today, the fundamentals are solid.

**The top 3 priorities that will have the most impact:**

1. **Content volume is the #1 bottleneck.** With only 4 indexed pages, the site has almost zero keyword surface area. Competitors like ThreadReaderApp have thousands of indexed pages (one per unrolled thread). You need to publish 8–12 blog posts in the next 30 days to start competing for long-tail keywords.
2. **Internal linking is nearly absent.** The blog posts link back to the homepage but don't cross-link to each other. This wastes link equity and makes it harder for Google to understand your topic clusters.
3. **No backlink profile exists yet.** As a new domain with zero referring domains, you need a deliberate link-building strategy (GitHub README links, Product Hunt launch, Obsidian community posts, dev blog mentions) to establish any domain authority.

**Overall assessment:** Strong foundation, needs aggressive content and link-building to gain traction. The niche is low-competition with clear keyword gaps — this is a winnable fight, but you have to move fast before Chrome extensions and GitHub tools absorb the search demand.
